    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions about your skin or how DermaGuide works? We’ve got you covered.

    How does DermaGuide detect my skin concern?

    Our AI model evaluates your responses and identifies common concerns like acne, pigmentation, or dryness using dermatology-backed logic. No photos are required — just simple answers.

    Do I need to know my skin type before using DermaGuide?

    Not at all! If you’re unsure about your skin type, our guided questionnaire and AI assistant help you discover it step by step with written and video-based guidance.

    Are the product recommendations dermatologist-approved?

    Yes, every recommendation is curated based on dermatologist-reviewed ingredients and brand safety data to ensure suitability for sensitive, oily, and dry skin types.

    Does DermaGuide save my personal data or skin report?

    No, your inputs are used only for generating real-time results. We value your privacy — no images or data are permanently stored on our servers.

    Can I access my skincare routine later?

    Yes! After your analysis, you can download your routine plan or have it sent via email for future reference.

    Is DermaGuide free to use?

    Yes, all basic features are free — including AI skin concern detection and product recommendations. Premium features like expert video tips are optional.
