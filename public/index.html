    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions about your skin or how DermaGuide works? We've got you covered.

    How does DermaGuide detect my skin concern?

    Our AI model evaluates your inputs and identifies common concerns like acne, pigmentation, or dryness using dermatology-backed logic. No photos are required — just simple answers.

    Do I need to know my skin type before using DermaGuide?

    Not at all! If you're unsure, our guided questionnaire helps you discover your skin type step by step with clear explanations along the way.

    Are the product recommendations dermatologist-approved?

    Yes, every recommendation is curated based on dermatologist-reviewed ingredient data to ensure suitability for sensitive, oily, dry, and combination skin types.

    Does DermaGuide save my personal data?

    Your inputs are used only for generating real-time results. We value your privacy — no images or personal data are permanently stored on our servers without your consent.

    Can I access my skincare routine later?

    After your analysis, you can screenshot or print your routine. A save-to-account feature is coming soon in our next update.

    Is DermaGuide free to use?

    Yes, all core features are completely free — including AI skin concern detection and product recommendations. Advanced features like CNN face scan analysis are in development.
